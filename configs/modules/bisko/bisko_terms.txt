BISKO (Municipal CO2 Balancing System) - Complete Terms and Abbreviations List

Administrative Terms
- AGS: Amtlicher Gemeindeschlüssel (Official municipality code)
- Gemeindename: Municipality name
- Kreis: District/County
- Bilanzjahr: Balance/accounting year
- Jahr: Year
- Gebietsstand: Territory status
- BISKO: Bilanzierungs-Systematik Kommunal (Municipal CO2 Balancing System)

Vehicle Categories (Fz-Kat = Fahrzeugkategorie)
- MZR: Motorisierte Zweiräder (Motorized two-wheelers - motorcycles, mopeds)
- Pkw: Personenkraftwagen (Passenger cars)
- LNF: Leichte Nutzfahrzeuge (Light commercial vehicles)
- Lkw: Lastkraftwagen (Trucks/Heavy goods vehicles)
- Bus: Linien- und Reisebus (Scheduled and touring buses)
- Linienbus: Scheduled/public transit buses
- Reisebus: Tour/charter buses
- Tram: Trams/Streetcars
- SSU: Schienengebundener Stadtverkehr inkl. U-Bahn (Rail-bound urban transport including subway)

Road Categories (SK = Straßenkategorie)
- AB: Autobahn (Highway/Motorway)
- AO: Außerorts (Out-of-town/Rural roads)
- IO: Innerorts (Within town/Urban roads)
- alle: All road types

Rail Transport Categories
- SPNV: Schienenpersonennahverkehr (Regional rail passenger transport)
- SPFV: Schienenpersonenfernverkehr (Long-distance rail passenger transport)
- SGV: Schienengüterverkehr (Rail freight transport)

Other Transport Modes
- Binnenschiff: Inland shipping/waterway transport
- Luft (LTO): Air transport (LTO = Landing and Take-Off cycle)

Fuel/Energy Types (Treibstoff)
- Benzin fossil: Fossil gasoline/petrol
- Benzin Bio: Bio-gasoline
- Diesel fossil: Fossil diesel
- Diesel Bio: Biodiesel
- Erdgas: Natural gas (CNG)
- Biogas: Biogas
- LPG: Liquefied Petroleum Gas (Autogas)
- Strom: Electricity
- Kerosin: Kerosene/Jet fuel

Propulsion Types
- Elektro: Electric
- Diesel: Diesel

Units of Measurement
- Mio. Fz-km: Million vehicle-kilometers
- Mio. Zug-km: Million train-kilometers
- MWh: Megawatt hours
- Gesamt: Total

Key German Terms
- Gemeindefeine Fahrleistungen: Municipal-specific driving performance
- Straßenverkehr: Road traffic
- Endenergieverbräuche: Final energy consumption
- Schiene, Wasser, Luft: Rail, water, air (transport modes)
- Antriebsart: Drive/propulsion type
